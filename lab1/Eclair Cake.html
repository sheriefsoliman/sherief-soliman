ECLAIR CAKE

Ingredients

-2 (3.5 ounce) packages instant vanilla pudding mix

-1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed

-3 cups milk

-1 (16 ounce) package graham cracker squares

-1 (16 ounce) package graham cracker squares

Directions

*In a medium bowl, thoroughly blend the pudding mix, whipped topping, and milk.

*Arrange a single layer of graham cracker squares in the bottom of a 13x9 inch baking pan.

*Evenly spread half of the pudding mixture over the crackers.

*Top with another layer of crackers and the remaining pudding mixture. Top with a final layer of graham crackers.

*Spread the frosting over the whole cake up to the edges of the pan. Cover, and chill at least 4 hours before serving.
